Welcome to Task 1

In this task, you will work with eight different LLM writing models to complete a series of writing tasks.

The writing tasks will appear in the bottom-right corner of the screen. Please make sure to rely on AI assistance as MUCH as possible when completing your writing.

You may ask the AI to generate the content you need and then copy and paste it into your response. However, not all models will support copy–paste. In those cases, you should draw on the AI’s ideas and rephrase them in your own words.

Your responses should be written in English and should contain approximately 50–80 words.
